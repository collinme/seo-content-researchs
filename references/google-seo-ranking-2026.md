## Core Update Accelerated Cycle

- Updates every ~6 weeks (from 3-4/year historically)
- March 2026: 80% TOP3 positions changed, 24% of TOP10 dropped out of top 100
- May 2026: 79.5% of sites with 5-year-old URL targets shifted
- Signal: **Original sources > aggregators** — government/nonprofit trust signals now outweigh credential-heavy content
- Signal: **Entity Health** — sites without verifiable entities classified as "unhelpful"

## NavBoost (Internal Google System)

Confirmed by 2024 API leak despite Google denying use of click data:

| Signal | What it measures |
|--------|-----------------|
| goodClicks | User finds what they want, stays |
| badClicks | User clicks back to SERP quickly |
| lastLongestClicks | The final page user spends most time on |

## AI Content Policy (2026)

| Status | What Google says |
|--------|-----------------|
| ✅ Allowed | AI-assisted content with human review and editing |
| ❌ Penalized | Scaled automated production (regardless of AI/human) |
| ❌ Penalized | Expired domain abuse |
| ❌ Penalized | Site reputation abuse ("parasite SEO") |
| Required | AI disclosure + human review + original value + author info |

## Google's 32 Self-Assessment Questions

Bucket 4 (search-engine-first red flags — answering "yes" is problematic):
1. **"Are you using extensive automation to produce content?"** — AI bulk production risk
2. **"Are you primarily summarizing others without adding value?"** — AI stitched content
3. **"Are you entering a niche without expertise?"** — AI filler site
